Platform features

Everything your operation needs

From roster planning to financial close — built for the pace of nightlife.

📋

Roster Planning

Assign PR talent to shifts across outlets. Manage weekly rosters with real-time availability tracking, conflict detection, and multi-outlet swap requests.

⏱️

Live Shift Tracking

GPS-assisted check-in, selfie verification, and live floor view. See every PR's status — scheduled, en-route, on-duty, or completed — at a glance.

🧾

Receipt Scanning

PR talent scans receipts on-shift via camera OCR or manual entry. Drinks, tips, and table commissions are auto-calculated by tier rates in real time.

💳

Payment Vouchers

Agency finance heads issue e-signed payment vouchers per payroll cycle. PRs review, sign digitally, or raise disputes directly through the portal.

📊

Sales & PNL Dashboard

Outlets track live drink sales, table revenue, and shift PNL. Agency sees cross-outlet reconciliation with variance reporting every week.

🔄

Weekly Reconciliation

Three-way confirmation between agency, outlet, and PR closes each payroll week. Variance disputes are tracked with audit trail and resolution history.

💰

Collection Invoicing

Agencies auto-generate collection invoices for outlets — payroll passthrough, commission splits, and platform fees with aging and overdue tracking.

⭐

Ratings & KPI

Outlets rate PRs after every shift. Agency tracks KPI tiers, attendance percentage, and consecutive low-rating flags to maintain talent quality.

🌟

Special Services

Book ad-hoc services — brand activations, private events, table hostessing — with tripartite approval between agency, outlet, and PR talent.

How it works

One platform, three portals

Agency, Outlet, and PR each have a tailored view — all synced in real time.

1

Onboard your roster

Add PR talent with full profiles — IC, training tier, languages, KPI history, and portfolio photos. Approve sign-up applications or invite directly.

2

Plan weekly shifts

Create shift plans for each outlet. Assign PRs by tier, set dress codes, and configure per-tier wage + commission rates for every shift.

3

Monitor live operations

Track check-ins, floor sales, late flags, and SOS incidents in real time. Broadcast messages or shift changes directly to PRs on duty.

4

Issue payroll & reconcile

Finance head e-signs payment vouchers per cycle. Review outlet sales against PV totals, confirm weekly reconciliation, and collect from outlets.

1

Set up your workspace

Configure your venue's drink menu, commission rates, and PR tier pay scales. Link to your agency for seamless roster coordination.

2

View tonight's roster

See which PRs are assigned, confirmed, and arriving for your shift. Monitor en-route status and check-in times live.

3

Log floor sales

Record drink units, table sales, and per-drink prices throughout the night. The PNL dashboard updates in real time as PRs scan receipts.

4

Confirm & pay

Review weekly reconciliation, confirm outlet-side figures, and settle agency invoices with your linked payment card.

1

Build your profile

Upload your ID, com card, and portfolio photos. Select languages and link to your agency. Your training tier unlocks higher pay rates.

2

Accept your shift

Receive shift offers from your agency with full details — venue, event, pay tier, and dress code. Accept or request a swap if needed.

3

Check in & track sales

Mark en-route, then check in with selfie and GPS. Scan receipts throughout the shift to log drinks, tips, and tables to your commission tally.

4

Get paid

Review your payment voucher, sign digitally, and receive your payout. Dispute line items directly if figures don't match your records.
